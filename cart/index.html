Rubber ducks are very important when taking a test because they are able to be talked to so you can figure out the answer. Talking to rubber ducks during a test can serve as a form of "rubber duck debugging," a technique where explaining a problem out loud helps clarify thoughts. By verbalizing your reasoning and answering questions posed to the duck, you can process information more thoroughly and identify gaps in your understanding. The act of talking out loud engages your brain in a different way, potentially leading to a deeper comprehension of the material
